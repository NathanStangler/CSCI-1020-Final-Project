Summary

The story follows Peter Parker, a teenage high school student who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. With these abilities, he fights crime in New York City as Spider-Man, while also struggling to balance his personal life and responsibilities as a student and friend.

Throughout the comics, Spider-Man faces off against various villains, such as Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and Venom. He also becomes a member of the Avengers and forms close relationships with other characters in the Marvel universe.

Popular Phrase

The most popular quote used in Spider-Man was said by Uncle Ben giving advice to Peter Parker.
