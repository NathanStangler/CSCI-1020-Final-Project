Summary

Jurassic Park is a science fiction franchise that began with the 1993 film of the same name. The franchise is based on the 1990 novel by Michael Crichton and directed by Steven Spielberg. The story takes place on an island where a billionaire and a small group of scientists are invited to tour a theme park where genetically cloned dinosaurs are the main attraction. However, the park's security system fails and the dinosaurs break free and start to hunt the visitors.

The franchise is known for its groundbreaking visual effects, suspenseful storytelling, and the iconic dinosaurs.
