Summary

Star Wars is a science fiction franchise that began with a film series created by George Lucas. The franchise includes movies, television shows, books, comics, and video games, and is set in a galaxy far, far away.

The main saga focuses on the battle between the Jedi, who use the power of the Force for good, and the Sith, who use the dark side of the Force for evil. The original trilogy, released between 1977 and 1983, tells the story of Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, and Han Solo as they fight against the evil Empire and Darth Vader.

The prequel trilogy, released between 1999 and 2005, explores the rise of Darth Vader and the fall of the Jedi Order.

The sequel trilogy, released between 2015 and 2019, follows the next generation of characters as they confront a new threat to the galaxy.
