Summary

Iron Man is a superhero character appearing in comic books published by Marvel Comics. The story follows Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who uses a powered suit of armor to protect the world as Iron Man. After being kidnapped and injured, Stark creates the suit to escape and subsequently uses it to protect the world and fight against evil. Throughout the comics, Stark faces off against various villains, both as Iron Man and in his civilian identity, and becomes a founding member of the Avengers, a superhero team.

Iron Man has been adapted for several films, television shows and video games, with the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) trilogy directed by Jon Favreau and starring Robert Downey Jr. as Stark/Iron Man, being the most successful and well-known adaptation. The trilogy began in 2008 and ended in 2013, with Stark's story arc concluded in Avengers: Endgame (2019).
